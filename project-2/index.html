Easy Homemade Beef Stroganoff Caserole

How to make Poor man's stroganoff

This recipe is a staple from my childhood. The meer mention of it brings back memories of dinner with my grandmother or dinner with my parents. Prep time is about 15 mins. and cook time is about 45 mins. But being a casserole it can also be prepped, then put in the fridge and baked anytime within the next several days.

Grandma Carole's Poorman's Stroganoff

You will need


  • 18 inch frying pan
  • Large pot for boiling
  • Large glass bowl or casserole dish
  • Large pot or bowl for mixing
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 can cream of chicken
  • 1 can cream of mushroom
  • 1 can of corn or green beans
  • 8 oz sour cream
  • 2 cups large elbow macaroni or large shell noodles
  • 1 bag of shredded cheddar cheese (8 oz)

The recipe itself is super easy and has a few huge perks:

  1. Cheap: All together the ingredients should cost around $10.
  2. Customizable: The recipe can be altered or seasoned to fit anyones preferences.
  3. Size: The recipe makes enough to feed 5 or 6 people depending on portion size.

If you make a side the meal will stretch even further. Any leftovers can be put in the fridge, because of the simple ingredients it tastes just as good reheated as it did fresh.
