Grandma Carol's Poorman's Stroganoff

-Lachina Family Cookbook

The Story

Poorman's Stroganoff is a simplified southern classic from the Lachina family, its been made for the last four generations of the family. It originated from my great great grandmother, Beverly Lachina.

Time
Prep 15 mins
Cook 45 mins
Total 1 Hour

Ingredients

  1 lb. ground beef
  1 can cream of chicken
  1 can cream of mushroom
  1 can of corn or green beans
  8 oz sour cream
  2 cups large elbow macaroni or large shell noodles
  1 bag of shredded cheddar cheese (8 oz)

Directions

  1. Brown beef
  2. Boil noodles in water
  3. preheat oven to 350 degrees 
    (skip this step if you plan to serve it at a later time)
  4. While those cook, stir together the rest of the ingredients (excluding the cheese) in a large pot or mixing bowl
  5. Stir beef and pasta in with the rest of the ingredients
  6. Pour mixed indgredients into large casserole pan and spread cheese evenly across top to form layer
  7. Put in oven for 45 mins or until cheese is melted and bubly 
    (If you plan to cook it later simply cover it and place it in the fridge,
it should be good for several days if kept cold)
  8. Let cool and settle on countertop for 10 mins before serving, then enjoy!
